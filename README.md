# ULTRAReplay
Record and rewatch your ULTRAKILL gameplay from a new perspective.

ULTRAReplay lets you record,edit,and export your ULTRAKILL gameplay. . .
It is a work in progress,nothing has been done yet but that will change very very soon. . .

Proof of concept : https://github.com/bitmotte/ULTRAReplay-Legacy



<details>
<summary>Run Instructions. . .</summary>
  There are no released builds yet. . . you must build yourself.
  
  See build instructions to know how,then come back to see how to run the mod.




  . . .If you have built the project, very good. . .
  
  navigate from project root : ULTRAReplay (root) > bin > Debug > netstandard2.1 . . .
  
  Then copy bitmotte.ULTRAReplay.dll to BepInEx plugins folder,if you have not had BepInEx installed here is a guide : https://docs.bepinex.dev/articles/user_guide/installation/index.html

  You are done. . . you can now run ULTRAKILL and try the mod. . .
</details>
